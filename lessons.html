THE MENU

Philly Cheesesteak

The Philly Cheesesteak starts with freshly grilled proteins and vegetables piled on an authentic hearth-baked roll that comes from a bakery in Philadelphia. We toast every roll to order before adding 4 slices of Swiss/American cheese. It’s a perfect balance of freshness in every bite. And for those nay-sayers who try to tell us an authentic cheesesteak comes with cheese-wiz. Best in KC!

Nachos

We ain't talking about a cheap $5 box of warmed-over plate here. We’re talking grilled proteins, grilled vegetables, liquid-gold cheese sauce, beans, toppings, house-made salsas & ranch or sour cream — all loaded on tortilla chips. Matter of fact, it’s so Fire that we ship the chips separately to avoid the sog-out effect during the drive.

Burritos

Our burrito start with a bed of citrus-cilantro rice mixed with a house seasoning blend wrap them up in a delicious flour tortilla. They come with your choice of black or pinto beans, grilled protein & vegetables, and selections of toppings, dressings, and sauces. We say GO BIG and GO HOME!
